Privacy Policy for SnapGroup

Introduction

Welcome to SnapGroup! Your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. SnapGroup is a camera app that provides smart template recommendations to capture stunning group photos effortlessly.

Information We Collect

Camera Permissions

SnapGroup requires access to your device's camera to take photos. We do not store or transmit any photos taken by the app to our servers or third parties.

Photos Permissions

The app needs access to your device's photo library to save the pictures you take. We do not access or collect any other photos in your library.

Local Storage

We use local storage on your device to save app settings and preferences. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or third parties.

How We Use Your Information

- Camera Access: To enable the functionality of taking photos within the app.

- Photos Access: To save the photos you capture directly to your photo library.

- Local Storage: To store app settings and preferences, ensuring a personalized user experience.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

SnapGroup does not collect, share, or sell any personal data. We do not transmit any of your data to our servers or any third parties.

Ads and Microtransactions

- SnapGroup is completely ad-free.

- There are no in-app purchases or microtransactions.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your data. Since we do not collect personal data, the primary focus is on maintaining the integrity of your device's local storage where app settings and preferences are saved.

User Rights

Since SnapGroup does not collect personal data, there are no specific data rights related to accessing, correcting, or deleting your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

About SnapGroup

Capture stunning group photos effortlessly with SnapGroup! Our app provides smart template recommendations based on the number of people in your photos, ensuring balanced and symmetrical compositions using grids like the rule of thirds, radial, and golden ratio. With advanced face detection, SnapGroup ensures everyone is perfectly positioned within the template, so no one gets cut out. Say goodbye to awkward group photos and hello to beautifully composed memories with SnapGroup!

Key Features:

  • Smart template recommendations for balanced group photos
  • Uses rule of thirds, radial, and golden ratio grids
  • Advanced face detection for perfect positioning
  • Easy to use and intuitive interface
